### # Research Profile: Jensen Huang

Jensen Huang is a renowned entrepreneur, investor, and technologist who has made significant contributions to the fields of artificial intelligence, game development, and high-performance computing. With a career spanning over two decades, Huang has established himself as one of the most influential figures in the technology industry.

**## Executive Summary**

Jensen Huang is the co-founder, chairman, and CEO of NVIDIA Corporation, a leading provider of graphics processing units (GPUs) and high-performance computing hardware. Under his leadership, NVIDIA has revolutionized the field of artificial intelligence (AI) by developing innovative technologies such as deep learning frameworks, GPUs, and tensor processing units (TPUs). His vision and strategic direction have enabled NVIDIA to become a major player in the global technology industry, with a market capitalization of over $500 billion.

**## Career Milestones**

* **Early Career (1995-1998)**
 + Co-founded NVIDIA Corporation with Chris Malachowsky and Curtis Priem
 + Developed the first NVIDIA graphics processing unit (GPU)
* **CEO, President, and CTO (1998-2005)**
 + Led NVIDIA's rapid growth and expansion into new markets
 + Developed the company's first high-performance computing products
* **CEO and CTO (2005-2014)**
 + Oversaw the development of NVIDIA's Tegra and Tesla processors
 + Expanded the company's presence in the gaming industry
* **CEO (2014-Present)**
 + Led NVIDIA's strategic direction and expansion into new markets
 + Developed and executed several major acquisitions, including Mellanox and DeepInstinct
* **Current Achievements**

* **NVIDIA Corporation**
 + Market capitalization: over $500 billion
 + Revenue: over $20 billion
 + Employees: over 18,000
* **Awards and Recognition**
 + Received the 2013 MIT Technology Review's "Lifelong Tech Innovator"
 + Named one of the "100 Most Influential People in the World" by Time magazine (2017)
 + Received the 2018 Ernst & Young "Entrepreneur of the Year" award

**## Current Impact & Ventures**

Jensen Huang's leadership at NVIDIA has had a significant impact on the industry and the world at large. His innovative approach to AI and computing has enabled the development of cutting-edge technologies that have transformed industries such as gaming, scientific computing, and data analytics.

Huang has also been a prominent advocate for open-source software development and has been involved in several initiatives to promote the adoption of GPU technology. In addition, he has been a vocal critic of the lack of diversity and inclusion in the tech industry and has used his platform to raise awareness about social and economic issues.
